## The Problem

A cybersecurity marketplace needed to rank for hundreds of vendor and solution-specific keywords — but manually creating quality content for 300+ vendors was impossible at the speed the market required.

The challenge: cybersecurity buyers search for very specific solutions ("SIEM vendors," "endpoint detection tools," "zero trust network access"), and generic content doesn't convert. Each page needed to be genuinely useful.

## The Solution

Built a programmatic SEO system using AI to generate, structure, and publish vendor and solution pages at scale — while maintaining quality through structured templates and human review workflows.

Key components:
- **Content architecture** defining the schema for vendor pages, solution pages, and category pages
- **AI generation pipeline** using LLMs to draft vendor summaries, capability breakdowns, and use cases from structured data
- **Quality review workflow** ensuring AI-generated content met accuracy and helpfulness standards before publishing
- **Internal linking strategy** creating a topic cluster structure that distributed authority across the 300+ pages

## The Results

- 300+ vendor and solution pages published
- 40% of targeted keywords achieved page 1 rankings within the first month
- 500+ vendor/solution keywords targeted across the programmatic content
- Established the marketplace as an authoritative resource in the cybersecurity vendor landscape
